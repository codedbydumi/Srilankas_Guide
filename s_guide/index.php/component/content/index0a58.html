Armed Forces In Sri-Lanka

Sri Lanka Army

 

The Sri Lankan Army is the oldest and largest of the Sri Lanka Armed Forces. Established as the Ceylon Army in 1949, it was renamed when Sri Lanka became a republic in 1972...

Sri Lanka Air Force

 

The Sri Lanka Air Force is the air arm and the youngest of the Sri Lanka Armed Forces. It was founded in 1951 as the Royal Ceylon Air Force with the assistance of the Royal Air Force. The SLAF played a major role throughout the Sri Lankan Civil War.

More Information-Sri Lanka Air Force - Wikipedia

 

Sri-Lanka Navy

 

The Sri Lanka Navy is the naval arm of the Sri Lanka Armed Forces and is classed as the country's most vital defence force due to its island geography and is responsible for the maritime defense of the Sri Lankan nation and its interests...

Special Task Force

 

The Special Task Force is an elite para-military forces unit of the Sri Lanka Police specializing in counter-terrorism and counter-insurgency operations. It was formed in 1983 not as a military force, but rather as a highly specialised police unit...
